Frequent Words with Mismatches Problem: Find the most frequent k-mers with mismatches in a string. Input: A string Text as well as integers k and d. (You may assume k ≤ 12 and d ≤ 3.) Output: All most frequent k-mers with up to d mismatches in Text. Sample Input: ACGTTGCATGTCGCATGATGCATGAGAGCT 4 1 Sample Output: GATG ATGC ATGT
